Visualization of Air Traffic in World's Busiest Airports

Visualization of 24 hours of flights arriving to and departing from the busiest airports in the world. The visualizations I have produced highlight the distinctive pattern that air traffic produces in each airport.

Most Common Country of Origin of Immigrants in the United States

I have analyzed a dataset of legal immigration produced by the Department of Homeland Security to generate a visualization where each US state is represented as the flag of the country the most of its immigrants come from.

This project gained worldwide attention and it has been mentioned by news outlets including Voice of America, Business Insider, and Bank of America Merrill Lynch.

WinYourMatchup.com

I am the CTO and one of the two founders of WinYourMatchup.com, a website that allows League of Legend players to search for a specific matchup in a specific lane, and find videos of pros or high elo streamers playing it.

The goal of the website is to help League of Legends players learn how to play their champions against every single possibile lane opponent, by watching the very best players in the world play the same matchup on their vods.

Application for the analysis of an unconstrained dataset of real-time collected tweets

Development of my UIC Master Thesis focused on the development of a method for the analysis and visualization of an unconstrained dataset of tweets. The method includes flux analysis, content analysis (dynamic tag clouds evolving over time), sentiment analysis, analysis of patterns of mentions and retweets (directed graphs evolving over time and geo-visualization of tweets. The method has been implemented in an application written in Java and Processing.

The application was used to analyze the flux, content and sentiment of tweets during the 2013 Italian Elections, allowing me to make very interesting predictions.
